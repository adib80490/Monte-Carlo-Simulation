In this example we will do a Monte Carlo simulation with an LC oscilator composed of an inductor and a capacitor. This circuit will resonate at a frequency given by the formula where L is the inductance of the coil in Henry and C is the Capacitance of the capacitor in Farad. In our simulation we will treat L and C as random variables that will have values within the given tolerance. We will generate the random values of L and C, calculate the resonant frequency and place it in a bin. The resulting graph will show us the incidence of each bin and the expected distribution of the population. We use d3.js to dynamically generate the resulting bargraph.
